

\vspace*{3cm}

{\par\centering {\Large \textbf{Abstract:}}\par}
\vspace{-2em}
\rule[-11pt]{\textwidth}{1pt}
\rule{\textwidth}{0.5pt}
    
We introduce the notion of a contramodule over a cocommutative coalgebra in a presentably symmetric monoidal $\infty$-category $\C$, and prove a version of Positselski's comodule-contramodule correspondence when the coalgebra is coidempotent. This gives a perspective, and a new proof of local duality---in the sense of Hovey--Palmieri--Strickland and Dwyer--Greenlees---whenever $\C$ is stable and compactly generated. As a result, we describe the category of $\Kpn$-local spectra as contramodules over the monochromatic sphere.
