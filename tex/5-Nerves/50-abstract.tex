

\begin{abstract}
    We construct a topological embedding of the homological spectrum of a big $tt$-category $\C$, into the Balmer spectrum of a stable enhancement of the Freyd envelope of $\C$. As any subspace of a $T_0$-space is $T_0$, this provides a positive answer to Balmers nerves of steel conjecture. 
\end{abstract}
